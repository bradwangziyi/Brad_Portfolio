MALIC: FIRST AID CRYO-CONTAINER

Project MALIC — Overview

In an emergency medical situation, a first aid cryo-container is a critical tool for preserving organs and tissues. I designed and developed the Modular Acute Limb Cryo-chamber (MALIC) system to provide the necessary sterility and refrigeration to preserve severed limbs of arbitrary size until they can be reattached.

Project Development

The inspiration came from a real factory workplace and a real incident that occurred there. The project participated in the International Dyson Award 2024.
